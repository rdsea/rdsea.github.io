One funded full time PhD or post-doc position for Engineering Analytics
of BigData  in IoT, Edge and Clouds Systems.

We are looking for  an excellent full time PhD student and/or post-doc positions
to join the Aalto Systems and Services Engineering Analytics ((http://rdsea.github.io) at
the Department of Computer Science, Aalto University, Finland.

A PhD student will be for 4 years and the post-doc position is limited  to
two years (renewable subject to available funding). The persons hired
will research engineering analytics for big data systems, including intelligent
incident monitoring and analysis, DevOps for big data systems, quality of analytics management,
and hybrid computing systems
for big data. The research will be carried out in connection with
possible national and EU research projects. In addition to research work, the persons
hired are expected to participate in teaching following the standard practices at
the department in the areas of IoT, edge computing, cloud computing, big data, and service computing.


The accepted candidates will be employed full time by Aalto University.

Potential candidates should possess
   - For PhD students: Eligibility for enrolling to PhD programs at Aalto University
     (see https://into.aalto.fi/display/endoctoralsci/Science)
   - For Postdoc: a  PhD in computer science
   - Excellent knowledge and experience of how to work in an EU/International research project.
   - Excellent knowledge of IoT, edge  and  cloud services
   - Excellent knowledge in distributed systems with a focus on middleware/platforms
   - Excellent programming skills  (with Java, Javascript, Python, Go, etc.)
   - Excellent scientific communication and writing skills in English
   - For postdoc position: strong publications in top conferences
   - Strong experience in developing software prototypes with DevOps tools
   - Excellent knowledge in working with industrial big data and cloud systems,
    like Google, Azure and Amazon Cloud, Big Query, Apache Hadoop, Apache Nifi, Kafka, Spark, etc.
   - Strong international teamwork experience

Prospective candidates should contact and send his/her applications,
including a motivation letter, CV & relevant certificates, transcript of academic records,
links to online PhD/master thesis and scientific papers, and a statement on
software prototype development experience.
